Glasgow on the go: Visualising Urban Mobility in Glasgow

by Danial Sheikh

Glasgow is a bustling and vibrant city, the way people navigate their way around it has a huge impact on important matters such as traffic jams to air pollution levels. The purpose of this application is to visualise urban mobility in Glasgow in a clear way for users. I believe I have represented the data in a visually appealing way whilst also making it easy to analyse for a user.
