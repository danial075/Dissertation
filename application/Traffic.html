Glasgow Traffic Data

This page shows the traffic movement in Glasgow for certain locations using the data and sensors from Glasgow open data hub. Please input the two dates from when you want to view the date. For this the dates must not be the same. Please note the earliest date you can enter 1st January 2019 and the latest being the current date when you are on the page.
